OutlineItem¶

The individual item of outline. The first item in an outline is obtained by Outline.get_first.

Methods¶

get_down Get the outline item’s immediate children in the hierarchy.
get_next Get next outline item at the same level as this item
get_page Get the page number of the destination.
get_page_flags Get the validity and meaning of the destination link, see fz_link_flag
get_page_lt Get the top left corner of the destination bounding box.
get_page_rb Get the bottom right corner of the destination bounding box.
get_title Get title of outline item using UTF-8 encoding.

get_page_flags(self)¶

Get the validity and meaning of the destination link, see fz_link_flag.

The flags is a bitfield consisting of fz_link_flag describing the validity and meaning of the different parts of lr and rb. Link destinations are constructed (as far as possible) so that lt and rb can be treated as a bounding box, though the validity flags indicate which of the values was actually specified in the file.

See also

Link.get_page_flags

Return type:int
get_page_lt(self)¶

Get the top left corner of the destination bounding box.

Warning

For the returning point, (0, 0) is in the bottom left corner, which is different from Rect and BBox.

Return type:Point
get_page_rb(self)¶

Get the bottom right corner of the destination bounding box.

Warning

For the returning point, (0, 0) is in the bottom left corner, which is different from Rect and BBox.

Return type:Point
